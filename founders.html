Vipin Kumar

About

Vipin Kumar is the founder of Maths Pathshala and a passionate Mathematics educator with strong academic and professional experience. He has worked as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) at Physics Wallah (PW), contributing to high-quality content and helping students understand complex concepts in a simple way.

He specializes in teaching Mathematics from Class 8 to 12, focusing on building strong conceptual foundations. His teaching style breaks down difficult problems into easy steps, making Maths more intuitive and less stressful.

With years of experience, he understands student learning patterns and common mistakes, allowing him to design structured, exam-oriented and concept-driven courses.

🎯 Vision

To make Mathematics simple, engaging, and accessible for every student, and remove the fear associated with the subject.

🚀 Mission

To provide concept-based learning, structured guidance, and continuous support so that every student can achieve academic excellence.
